Discord bot Privacy Policy

MTGCompanion operates free chatbots that you can install on Discord.

If you install MTGCompanion’s chat bots on your server, the bot will listen to your channels to determine if they need to respond to a message. Messages are sent to MTGCompanion’s server over an encrypted connection for automated inspection, by MTGCompanion Server

The Data
Data's content : MESSAGE,SENDER,TIMESTAMP,CHANNELID
Data are stored on encrypted disk space on ARM Debian Linux
MTGCompanion administrator is able to see messages sent to our bots for a period of less than 30 days. After this time, Bot discards this data

For any question, ask mtgdesktopcompanion@gmail.com Advices
For your safety, please do not install our chatbots on servers where vitally secret, confidential, tactical, or life-threatening information is discussed.

Take control
The bot listens to choosed private or public text channels. If you no longer wish to use the bot you can kick it from your server.
